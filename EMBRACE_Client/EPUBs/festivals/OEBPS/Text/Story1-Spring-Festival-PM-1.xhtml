Spring Festival

The Spring Festival is the most important traditional festival in China.

The Spring Festival lasts about 20 days at the end of the old year and the beginning of the New Year.

During the Spring Festival, the picture of the Kitchen God is near the stove.

The mother brings the treats on the red plate to the Kitchen God.

She prays that the whole family will have good food for the next year.

Then, the family starts to clean the house. (change scene so that mother, daughter, and son have feather dusters in kitchen).

They sweep away the dust.

They also hope to sweep away bad luck and to greet a happy new year.
