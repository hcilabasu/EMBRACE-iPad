Rosa went to the living room and said, "Paco, did you take the silver spoon?"

Paco ran out to the tree and barked.

He barked and barked, and the silver spoon dropped to the ground!

Paco picked up the spoon and carried it to Rosa.

Rosa said, "Paco, you are a bad dog for stealing my silver spoon."

"You will have to stay outside all day."
