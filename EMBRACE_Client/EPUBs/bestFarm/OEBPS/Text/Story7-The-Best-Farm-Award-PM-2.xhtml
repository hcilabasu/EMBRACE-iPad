The judge walked to the corral door.

She wanted to see how fast the horse could run.

The horse ran as fast as the wind and stopped next to the judge.

The judge said to Farmer Manuel, "You have the best animals and the best farm in the country."

She gave Manuel the trophy.

It was the most beautiful trophy that the animals had ever seen.

All of the animals cheered because they had worked together to become the best farm!
