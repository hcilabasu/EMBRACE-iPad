Everyone Helps



The owl flew to the chicken and whispered in her ear.

The owl said, "We can all help each other."

Then the chicken hopped to the pig's pen and opened the gate.

The chicken said to the pig, "Eat a pumpkin, then help another animal."

The pig ran to the biggest pumpkin and ate it.

Then the pig took an apple to the horse.

The apple will clean the horse's teeth.
