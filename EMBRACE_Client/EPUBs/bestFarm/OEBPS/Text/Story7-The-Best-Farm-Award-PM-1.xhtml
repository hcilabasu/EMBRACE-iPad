The Best Farm Award

It was Sunday, the day the judge comes.

The judge walked to the cow.

She tasted the cow's milk from the bucket.

"Yum, that's really good milk," she thought.

Then the judge counted the chicken's eggs, "One, two, three, four!"

Next, the judge walked to the pig to see if he was fat.

She saw the pig and said, "You are the fattest pig in the world."
