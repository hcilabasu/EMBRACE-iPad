Sofia ran to get a notepad and pencil, and brought them to her mother.

"I will go fetch the ingredients from the market!" she said enthusiastically.

Sofia's mom put a list of ingredients into a tote bag.

She handed Sofia the tote to carry the supplies.

"You can buy whatever you want with the change," she said to Sofia while handing her forty pesos.

"Thanks!" Sofia replied as she ran out the door.
