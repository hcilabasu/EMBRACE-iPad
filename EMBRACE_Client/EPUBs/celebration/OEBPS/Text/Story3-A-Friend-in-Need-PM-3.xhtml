Almost all the cows were in the corral when a black calf ran in the opposite direction.

Sofia acted quickly and rode her horse around the calf.

The calf ran back to the herd and into the corral.

"Thank you, Sofia!" Emilio said with a smile on his face. "Without your help, I would still be chasing cows."

He then got off his horse and closed the corral gate behind his cattle.
